# The Good Developer's Guide

Welcome to the comprehensive guide on software development best practices.

## Goal

This guide aims to gather the practices, principles, and methodologies that lead to producing quality, maintainable, and scalable code.

We often hear about best practices in software development, but they are unfortunately too theoretical and usually require years of experience to be truly understood.

## About this guide

The practices presented in this guide are not necessarily the best or the most universal. This guide primarily reflects a personal vision of good practices that, in my opinion, can help you become a better developer.

<Callout type="warning">
  This guide may not apply to everyone or every organization. Prioritize the
  practices defined by your team before applying those from this guide.
</Callout>
